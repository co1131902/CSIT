Steelpan

At a young age, I joined a pan band called Potential Symphony. There I learnt the foundation of playing steelpan and of course grew to love it. I later joined another band called Courts Sound Specialist of Laventille and was able to be a part of their championship achievement in 2008 when they won the National Panorama competition, medium category after also winning it the previous year.
